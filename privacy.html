Tickydo Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 19.04.2025

At Tickydo, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information in our app.

Data Collection

Tickydo does not collect or store any personal data. All tasks and to-do list items are stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to or shared with any third-party services.

Local Storage

Any information you input into the app is stored directly on your device. We do not use cloud storage or any external servers to store your data.

No Sharing of Data

Since no personal information is collected, we do not share any data with third parties, nor do we have access to your data.

Third-Party Services

Tickydo does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising.

Security

The data stored on your device is protected by the security features of your phone. Please make sure your phone is password-protected or uses biometric authentication to prevent unauthorized access to the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If any significant changes are made, we will notify you within the app.
